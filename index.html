A Word From Our Managing Partner

"When I founded OptSol Consulting Group, I had one clear conviction: that businesses, NGOs, and public institutions in Uganda deserved world-class financial and advisory services — delivered by people who truly understand our local context."

Over the past 12 years, that conviction has shaped everything we do. We have invested in building a team of highly qualified professionals — Chartered Accountants, Certified Public Accountants, and sector specialists — who bring both technical depth and genuine dedication to every client engagement.

Our clients span ambitious startups, established enterprises, grassroots NGOs, multilateral donor-funded programmes, and local government authorities across Uganda and East Africa. What they share is a need for a financial partner who is honest, reliable, and focused on real outcomes.

Uganda's business environment continues to evolve rapidly. Tax laws change. Governance expectations rise. Digital transformation accelerates. OptSol's mission is to ensure our clients are always ahead of these shifts — protected, compliant, and positioned to grow.
